What is Local Storage?

localStorage lets the browser store data permanently.

Example:

localStorage.setItem("name", "Shahid");

Retrieve it:

const name = localStorage.getItem("name");
console.log(name); // Shahid

Remove it:

localStorage.removeItem("name");
